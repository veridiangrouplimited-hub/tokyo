Consular Services

The Mission provides a full range of consular services to Nigerian citizens and eligible foreign nationals. All services require prior appointment unless stated otherwise.

  1. Home
  2. Consular Services
⚠️

Appointment Required: All consular services require a prior appointment. Please contact the Mission or use the online booking system to schedule your appointment before visiting. Walk-in visits are accepted for emergency cases only.

Our Services

Select a service below to view detailed information including requirements, fees, and the application process.

🛂

Visa Services

Information on Nigerian entry visas for foreign nationals, including tourist, business, student, transit, and diplomatic visas. Learn about visa categories, fees, and the application process.

⏱ Processing: 5–10 working days
View Details
📘

Passport Services

Apply for a new Nigerian Standard/Enhanced passport, renew an expiring passport, or replace a lost or damaged passport. Biometric data collection is required for all applications.

⏱ Processing: 6–8 weeks
View Details
🆘

Emergency Travel Certificate

For Nigerian citizens who have lost their passport or travel documents and require urgent assistance to return to Nigeria. Available in genuine emergency situations.

⏱ Processing: Same day – 48 hours
View Details
📜

Document Authentication

Authenticate Nigerian-issued documents for use in Japan, including educational certificates, birth/marriage certificates, legal documents, and corporate papers.

⏱ Processing: 3–5 working days
View Details
✍️

Notarial Services

Witness signatures on statutory declarations, certify true copies of documents, administer oaths and affirmations, and provide other notarial services to Nigerians abroad.

⏱ Processing: Same day (by appointment)
View Details
🤝

Consular Assistance

Support and assistance for Nigerians in difficulty, including those who have been arrested, hospitalised, become victims of crime, or are facing other emergencies abroad.

⏱ Emergency response: Immediate
View Details
