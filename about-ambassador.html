Ambassador Extraordinary & Head of Mission

Barr. Mrs. Olumilua
Oluwayimika Ayotunwa

Ambassador of Nigeria to Japan

Barr. Mrs. Olumilua Oluwayimika Ayotunwa — popularly known as Oluwayimika Olumilua — is a distinguished Nigerian legal practitioner, public servant, and the Ambassadorial Representative of Nigeria to Japan. She was nominated from Ekiti State as part of Nigeria's ambassadorial appointments and subsequently posted to Tokyo, where she now leads the Nigerian Mission as Head of Mission.

A Barrister-at-Law by training and vocation, Ambassador Olumilua has cultivated a professional career at the intersection of law, public service, and civic leadership. Her legal expertise, combined with a deep commitment to community advancement and women's empowerment, has defined her public profile and established her as a respected voice in southwest Nigeria's professional and civic spheres.

Prior to her appointment to the Tokyo Mission, Ambassador Olumilua was publicly identified as the Southwest Zonal Coordinator of the NACCIMA Business Women Group (NAWORG) — the women's enterprise arm of the Nigeria Association of Chambers of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture. In this capacity, she championed initiatives aimed at strengthening women's participation in business, public procurement, and enterprise development across the Southwest geopolitical zone of Nigeria.

Her appointment to Japan reflects a career of sustained service, professional distinction, and community leadership. Positioned at the helm of one of Nigeria's most strategically important diplomatic missions in Asia, Ambassador Olumilua brings to the role a bridge-building disposition, a commitment to the welfare of Nigerians in the diaspora, and a vision for deepening the bilateral relationship between Nigeria and Japan across diplomatic, economic, cultural, and people-to-people dimensions.

Ambassador Olumilua is dedicated to the advancement of Nigeria's national interests, the promotion of bilateral trade and investment between Nigeria and Japan, and the delivery of accessible, responsive consular services to the thousands of Nigerians who call Japan home.

Background & Key Roles

  • Appointed as Nigeria's Ambassadorial Representative to Japan, nominated from Ekiti State
  • Southwest Zonal Coordinator, NACCIMA Business Women Group (NAWORG), Federal Republic of Nigeria
  • Spearheaded public procurement sensitisation and enterprise participation programmes for women in business across the Southwest zone
  • Active legal practitioner (Barrister-at-Law) with a career spanning law, public service, and community advancement
  • Advocate for women's economic inclusion, enterprise development, and civic participation
  • Named among Nigeria's ambassadorial nominees, subsequently posted to the Embassy in Tokyo, Japan

Ambassador's Message

It is my honour and privilege to serve as Ambassador of Nigeria to Japan. Nigeria and Japan share a relationship grounded in mutual respect, shared ambition, and decades of fruitful cooperation — and it is a relationship with enormous untapped potential that this Mission is committed to realising.

Japan is one of the world's most innovative and technologically advanced economies, and Nigeria is Africa's largest economy and most populous nation. The convergence of these two great nations holds extraordinary promise — in trade, technology transfer, investment, education, and cultural exchange. It is my mandate to deepen that partnership and to ensure that both countries benefit fully from what they can offer each other.

To our Nigerian community in Japan: this Mission exists for you. We are here to support you, protect your rights, and ensure that wherever you are in Japan, you remain connected to your homeland. I encourage every Nigerian in Japan to register with the Mission, engage with our programmes, and continue to represent our great nation with pride and distinction.

H.E. Barr. Mrs. Olumilua Oluwayimika Ayotunwa Ambassador & Head of Mission Embassy of Nigeria, Tokyo, Japan
