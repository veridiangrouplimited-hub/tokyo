Register with the Mission

All Nigerians resident in Japan are encouraged to register with the Embassy. It takes just a few minutes.

  1. Home
  2. Nigerians in Japan
  3. Registration
Your Rights & Safety

Why Register with the Mission?

Registration connects you to your Embassy. In the event of a crisis — natural disaster, civil unrest, hospitalisation, or any personal emergency — the Mission can only reach you if you are on our register. It is free, quick, and strongly encouraged for all Nigerians in Japan.

Emergency Contact

The Embassy can reach you instantly in a crisis — earthquake, hospitalisation, or civil emergency.

Priority Services

Registered Nigerians receive priority access to consular services and advance notice of policy changes.

Stay Informed

Receive official notices, community events, visa updates, and government announcements by email.

Civic Participation

Stay connected to voter registration exercises and diaspora participation in national elections.

Step-by-Step

How to Register

1

Download & Complete the Registration Form

Obtain the Nigerian Citizens Registration Form from the Embassy's Consular Section or by email request. Complete all fields including your full name, Nigerian passport number, address in Japan, occupation, and emergency contact details.

2

Gather Your Supporting Documents

Prepare copies of your valid Nigerian International Passport (bio-data page), one proof of address in Japan (utility bill, bank statement, or tenancy agreement), one recent passport-sized photograph, and any Japan residence permit or visa (if applicable).

3

Submit to the Consular Section

Deliver your completed form and documents in person (Mon–Fri, 9:00 AM–4:00 PM), by post to the Embassy address, or by email to consular.tokyo@foreignaffairs.gov.ng. Registration is entirely free of charge.

4

Receive Your Confirmation ✓

The Consular Section will confirm your registration by email within 5–7 working days. You will then begin receiving official Mission communications. Please notify the Embassy promptly if your address, passport, or contact details change.

Checklist

Documents Required

Completed Nigerian Citizens Registration Form
Copy of valid Nigerian International Passport (bio-data page)
Proof of address in Japan — utility bill, bank statement, or lease agreement
One (1) recent passport-sized photograph
Copy of Japan residence permit or visa (where applicable)
Valid email address for official Mission communications
