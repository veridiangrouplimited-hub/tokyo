Applicants who submitted passport renewal applications before 1 April 2026 and have not yet been contacted for collection are advised to call the Mission on +[Code] [Number] to confirm the status of their application.

ADVISORY Advisory to Nigerian Citizens: Safeguarding Travel Documents
10 April 2026

The Mission wishes to advise all Nigerian citizens in Japan to take adequate precautions to safeguard their travel documents, including passports and emergency travel certificates.

  • Keep your passport in a secure location at all times
  • Make a photocopy of your passport data page and keep it separately
  • Do not hand your passport to unauthorised persons
  • If your passport is lost or stolen, report it to the local police and the Mission immediately
  • Ensure your passport does not expire while you are abroad

For passport-related enquiries, contact the Mission at passport.tokyo@foreignaffairs.gov.ng.

SERVICE UPDATE Online Appointment Booking System Now Available
15 May 2026

The Mission is pleased to announce the launch of its new online appointment booking system for all consular services. Applicants can now book appointments directly through the Mission's website without needing to call or email.

To book an appointment, visit Consular Services and select the relevant service. You will receive an email confirmation of your appointment with instructions on what to bring.

ℹ️

Please ensure you book only one appointment per application. Duplicate bookings will be cancelled. Failure to attend a booked appointment without prior cancellation may result in a waiting period before a new appointment can be booked.

SERVICE UPDATE Passport Collection: New Tuesday & Thursday Schedule
5 May 2026

Effective immediately, completed passport collections will be processed on Tuesdays and Thursdays only, between 10:00 AM and 1:00 PM. Applicants will receive an SMS notification when their passport is ready for collection.

Please bring the original payment receipt and a valid form of identification when collecting. Processing on other days will not be possible.
