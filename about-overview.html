Visit the Mission

Our offices are open Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM (excluding public holidays). Consular services are strictly by appointment.

  • Book appointments via our online portal or by telephone
  • Bring all required documents to your appointment
  • Arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled time
  • Photo ID is required for entry to the Mission
