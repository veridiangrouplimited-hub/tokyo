Head of Mission
Message from the Ambassador

H.E. Barr. Mrs. Oluwayimika Olumilua

Ambassador & Head of Mission, Embassy of Nigeria, Tokyo

"It is my honour and privilege to serve as the Head of Mission of the Federal Republic of Nigeria to Japan. Nigeria and Japan share a relationship grounded in mutual respect, shared ambition, and decades of fruitful cooperation — with enormous untapped potential that this Mission is committed to realising.

To our Nigerian community in Japan: this Mission exists for you. I encourage every Nigerian in Japan to register with the Mission, engage with our programmes, and continue to represent our great nation with pride and distinction."

— H.E. Barr. Mrs. Olumilua Oluwayimika Ayotunwa
