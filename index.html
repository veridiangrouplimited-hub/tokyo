Message from the Ambassador

H.E. Barr. Mrs. Oluwayimika Olumilua

Ambassador & Head of Mission, Embassy of Nigeria to Japan

"

Nigeria and Japan share a relationship grounded in mutual respect, shared ambition, and decades of fruitful cooperation — with enormous untapped potential that this Mission is committed to realising.

To our Nigerian community in Japan: this Mission exists for you. I encourage every Nigerian in Japan to register with the Mission, engage with our programmes, and continue to represent our great nation with pride and distinction.

Trade & Investment Diaspora Engagement Bilateral Diplomacy

— H.E. Barr. Mrs. Olumilua Oluwayimika Ayotunwa
