Register with the Mission

Registration is free, confidential, and strongly recommended for all Nigerians in Japan.

All Nigerian citizens residing in Japan are strongly encouraged to register with the Mission. Registration enables the Mission to:

  • Contact you quickly in the event of an emergency, natural disaster, or security incident
  • Send you important alerts, advisories, and community notices
  • Assist you more effectively if you require consular assistance
  • Keep you informed about Mission events, services, and updates
  • Provide accurate counts of Nigerians in Japan for planning purposes
🔒

Your registration information is held securely and confidentially. It will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except where required by law or to protect your safety.

🆘 Emergency Support for Nigerians

The Mission is committed to protecting all Nigerian citizens in Japan. If you or another Nigerian is in danger, in difficulty, or requires urgent assistance, please contact us immediately.

We Can Help With:

  • 🆘 Lost or stolen passport / travel documents
  • ⚖️ Arrest or detention
  • 🏥 Serious illness or hospitalisation
  • 💀 Death of a Nigerian in Japan
  • ⚠️ Victims of crime or trafficking
  • ✈️ Emergency repatriation
  • 🆘 Natural disasters or civil unrest
